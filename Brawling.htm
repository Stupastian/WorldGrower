WorldGrower:Brawling

Brawling is initiated by asking some to brawl.
If the other character has no higher priority tasks to perform, it will agree.

While brawling, only non-lethal melee attacks are allowed.
The first character to have its hit points reduced to 1 loses.

It's possible to brawl for money or for the glory of winning.
Wearing gloves increases the damage dealt by non-lethal attacks, as will having a high hand-to-hand skill.
