WorldGrower:Thievery

All thievery actions use the thievery skill.
Most thievery actions are illegal if discovered.
The chance of success depends on the thievery skill level and how hard the action is.
  • Steal item: allows the character to steal an item from another character's inventory.
  • Steal gold: allows the character to steal gold from another character.
  • Reverse pickpocket: allows the character to slip an item into another character's inventory.
  • Unlock Container: allows the character to unlock a locked container with a lockpick in its left hand.
