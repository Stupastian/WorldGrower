WorldGrower:Alchemy

Most alchemy items are created at an apothecary, except for water which is collected at a drinking well.

IconNameDescription
waterDrinking water restores a character's water level.
wineDrinking wine restores a character's water level and may make it intoxicated.
poisonDrinking poison poisons a character, making it lose hit points for several turns.
bloodDrinking blood restores a vampire's blood level.
sleeping potionDrinking a sleeping potion puts a character to sleep for several turns.
healing potionDrinking a healing potion restores hit points.
cure poison potionDrinking a cure poison potion cures a character from being poisoned.
cure disease potionDrinking a cure disease potion cures a character from all diseases.
