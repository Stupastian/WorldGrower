WorldGrower:Governance

Elections

In order to access governance options, a character needs to become village leader.
First elections needs to be triggered using dialogue with another villager.
Doing this will create a voting box.
The character needs to register itself as a candidate with the voting box.
After the candidacy phase, the voting phase will start. All villagers can vote, and the character with the most votes becomes the new village leader.

Governing

After a character becomes village leader, it's possible to change the governance options:



The governance dialog indicates which actions are legal to perform.
It also shows income and expense for the village.

If the player character is the leader of the villagers, it's possible to change tax rates and wages.

It's also possible to restrict voting options or to change the number turns people have to become a candidate or to vote.
