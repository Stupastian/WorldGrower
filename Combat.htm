WorldGrower:Combat

Each unit has hit points (HP).
When hit points are reduced to 0, the unit is dead.
Each turn, a unit may attack another unit.
Damage done depends on attack skill, how fatigued a unit is and the target's damage resistance.

Attack Types

There are several basic attacks you can do, each depends on how you start your attack.

Melee attack

This is the default attack. To use it, you need to have a melee weapon in your left hand and choose the attack action.

Non-lethal melee attack

This attack is the same as a normal melee attack, except that the target cannot be killed.
If the target's hit points would be reduced to 0, they're reduced to 1 instead and the target becomes unconscious.

Ranged attack

To perform a ranged attack, you need a ranged weapon like a bow in your left hand.
